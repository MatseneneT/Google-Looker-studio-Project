# Google-Looker-studio-Project
This project transforms raw sales data into a strategic dashboard using Excel for data cleaning and Google Looker Studio for visualization. It provides a centralized view of market performance, allowing stakeholders to analyze customer segments, regional trends, and product category success to drive growth and operational efficiency.
## Data set used
-<a href="https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1j-SF2Spv8GBNEGrgX0uGwQ-W79HukJkA">Dataset</a>

## Objectives
- Eliminate redundancies and errors through rigorous cleaning and deduplication in Excel.
- Provide a high-level overview of business health using real-time KPIs (Total Orders, Customer Count, and Market Reach).
- Design an interactive interface that allows stakeholders to filter and drill down into specific regions, product categories, and shipping modes.
- Surface actionable insights regarding customer segmentation and seasonal sales patterns to optimize future operational strategies.

## Key Findings
- The Consumer segment is the primary driver of business volume, accounting for 51.9% of all orders, significantly outpacing the Corporate and Home Office segments.
- Technology is the highest-performing category, representing 60.3% of orders, followed by Office Supplies (21.2%) and Furniture (18.5%).
- Sales are highly concentrated in major hubs. New York City and Los Angeles lead the country in order volume, followed by Philadelphia and San Francisco.
- The West Region shows the highest engagement level at 32%, while the South represents the smallest market share at 16.2%.

## Recommendations and Conclusion 
Recommendations
-Implement targeted Q1 promotional campaigns to counteract the "February Slump" and stabilize annual revenue cycles.
- Replicate the high-performing West Region’s engagement model in the South to capture untapped market share within the Consumer segment.
- Streamline logistics for Standard Class shipping to reduce operational costs and maximize margins on the most-preferred customer delivery method.

  Conclusion
This project successfully transitioned raw data into an interactive Looker Studio dashboard, identifying the Consumer segment and Technology category as primary growth drivers. By establishing a high-integrity data foundation, the analysis provides actionable strategies to stabilize seasonal revenue and expand market share in underperforming regions, ensuring more efficient and data-driven operations.
